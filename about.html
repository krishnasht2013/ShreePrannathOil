Our Story

A journey of preserving tradition while embracing innovation

In 1995, Ram Prasad Ghimire started Shree Prannath Oil Mill with a simple yet profound mission: to provide the local farming community with honest, transparent oil pressing services while preserving the traditional methods passed down through generations.

What began as a small operation serving local farmers in Kathmandu valley has grown into Nepal's most trusted oil mill, serving over 500 farmer families across the country. Yet, despite our growth, we've never forgotten our roots or compromised on the quality and traditional methods that define us.

Today, led by the second and third generations of the Ghimire family, we continue to combine time-honored oil pressing techniques with modern quality control and hygiene standards, ensuring that every bottle of oil carries the pure, authentic taste of Nepal.
