Our Oil Pressing Process

A transparent, step-by-step process ensuring the highest quality oil extraction from your precious mustard seeds.

1

Seed Inspection & Registration

15-30 minutes

We carefully inspect your mustard seeds for quality, moisture content, and purity. Each batch is registered with your details.

2

Cleaning & Preparation

30-45 minutes

Seeds are cleaned to remove any impurities, stones, or damaged seeds to ensure optimal oil extraction.

3

Cold Pressing Process

1-2 hours

Traditional cold-pressing method that preserves nutrients and natural flavor without any heat or chemicals.

4

Filtration & Quality Check

30 minutes

Fresh oil is filtered through natural cloth filters and quality tested for purity and taste.

5

Packaging & Documentation

15-30 minutes

Oil is packaged in your preferred containers with complete documentation of quantity and quality metrics.
